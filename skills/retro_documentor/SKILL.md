# Universal Retro Documentor Engine: Surgical Dissection Standard

The **Universal Retro Documentor Engine** is an enterprise-grade, surgical auditing system designed to autonomically deconstruct any codebase into its lowest logical primitives. It moves beyond "documentation" into **"Architectural Autopsy"**, ensuring 100% visibility into every logic gate and state mutation.

---

## 🛡️ The Surgical Dissection Protocol

Every artifact (Project, Module, File) MUST follow the **Absolute 1-23 Surgical Standard**. No summarization is permitted.

### 🗺️ The Tri-Layer Architectural Mirror Protocol
The engine must establish a hierarchical shadow of the source tree at three distinct layers of resolution. **Every level (Project, Module, File) MUST follow the Absolute 1-23 Surgical Standard.** No summarization is permitted.

1.  **Layer 1: Application-Scope Surgical Audit (Root)**
    - **Location**: `documentation/README.md`.
    - **Scope**: Deconstruction of the entire application ecosystem from 1-23.
2.  **Layer 2: Module-Scope Surgical Audit (Folder Roots)**
    - **Location**: `documentation/{directory_path}/README.md`.
    - **Scope**: Deconstruction of the specific functional module from 1-23. Every folder in the target must receive an Audit.
3.  **Layer 3: File-Scope Surgical Audit (File Level)**
    - **Location**: `documentation/{directory_path}/{file_basename}.md`.
    - **Scope**: Exhaustive 23-section systemic deconstruction following the **Absolute 1-23 Surgical Standard**.

### 🛠️ Execution Protocol: Absolute Compliance
- **Audit Mandate**: "A summary is an omission." All artifacts must be analytical deconstructions.
- **Contextual Inversion**: High-level layers (1 and 2) must be generated first to ensure systemic anchoring.
- **Structural Parity**: The `documentation/` folder must exactly mirror the target's logical directory structure.
- **Mermaid Mandate**: Every Level 1 and 2 audit must include at least one architectural map.

### 1. The 1:1 Parity Mandate
- **Surgical Accuracy**: Every method/procedure in the source must have a corresponding entry in Section 19.
- **Zero Omission**: Every `if`, `else`, `switch`, `try/catch`, and `loop` must be identified and mapped in the Logical Branching Matrix.

### 2. Multi-Pass Analysis (The Audit Loop)
To ensure hyper-fidelity, the Documentor must analyze the target in three distinct passes:
1.  **Pass 0: Member & Signature Scan**: Map the metadata, scopes, and public/private surface.
2.  **Pass 1: Logic & Branching Audit**: Trace every control-flow path. Identify clinical/business rules embedded in logic gates.
3.  **Pass 2: Data Lifecycle & Mutation Scan**: Trace every field/variable mutation. Identify side effects, database interactions, and state changes.
